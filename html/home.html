Parking Using Ultrasonic Waves

Ultrasonic sensors use high-frequency sound waves to detect objects. These sensors help in identifying whether a parking spot is occupied or vacant. They are reliable, cost-effective, and easy to install, making them ideal for both indoor and outdoor parking facilities.

Applications include:

  • Detecting available parking spots
  • Guiding drivers to available spots
  • Automating ticketing and access control

Advantages:

  • Works in various conditions (rain, fog, dust)
  • Inexpensive compared to other technologies
  • Easy to install
